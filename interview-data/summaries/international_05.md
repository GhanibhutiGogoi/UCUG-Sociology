# international_05 summary

Group: international
Respondent label: Respondent 5

- International student from Macao in Year 2, preparing for Smart Manufacturing, with two years at HKUST(GZ).
- Found it very easy to make friends because the campus is small and clubs create contact.
- Feels a sense of social belonging and spends time with both Chinese and international students who share hobbies.
- Says hobbies and sports help connection, while academic life and beliefs make deeper conversation harder.
- Does not see language or culture as major personal barriers, but thinks students still remain in separate groups.
- Separates social life and academic life, though weak communication can mean missing information.
- Does not see a strong relationship between social networks and academic performance.
- Thinks UFUG emphasizes participation and group work more, while UCUG focuses more on grades and academics.
