# mainland_01 summary

Group: mainland
Respondent label: Respondent 1

- First-year undergraduate from Mainland China.
- Social environment perceived as average; made relatively few friends initially.
- Thinks Chinese and international students tend to stay in separate circles mainly because of language.
- Reports interacting with international students quite a lot.
- Believes UFUG courses are more difficult.
- Does not think international students are academically disadvantaged; sees classmates as friendly.
- Identifies language and culture as central barriers.
- Feels course selection and dormitory arrangements make integration harder.
