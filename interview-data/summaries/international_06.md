# international_06 summary

Group: international
Respondent label: Respondent 6

- International student from Macau, China, majoring in Artificial Intelligence, with two years at HKUST(GZ).
- Found it difficult to make friends initially because of introversion and differences in habits and lifestyles.
- Does not feel a strong sense of social belonging and mostly stays with students from the same background.
- Describes interaction with Chinese students as mostly surface-level.
- Says language is not a major issue with Chinese students, but English can be a barrier with some international students.
- Thinks cultural differences strongly affect comfort and confidence in communication.
- Believes social experience affects academic access, participation, information flow, and confidence.
- Feels more comfortable in UFUG than in UCUG and recommends more sustained, mixed interaction structures.
