# mainland_03 summary

Group: mainland
Respondent label: Respondent 3

- Second-year AI student with 1.5 years at HKUST(GZ).
- Reports a lot of interaction with international students and says friendship is easy.
- Believes students still stay in separate groups because same-country peers share more topics.
- Thinks good English and personality help interaction; introversion and discrimination hinder it.
- Sees language and culture differences as having a huge impact.
- Says UCUG performance is lower because of an unclear grading system.
- Does not think stronger social networks necessarily improve academic performance.
- Attributes weak integration to the low percentage of international students and few campus activities.
