1. Could you briefly introduce yourself — your nationality, program, year of study, and how long you have been at HKUST(GZ)?
I’m a international student from Macao, now a year 2 preparing to studying Smart manufactoring, I have been in HKUST(GZ) for 2 years

2. What was your first impression of student life at HKUST(GZ), especially socially?
Life is very good here, with a one-person bedroom, and dorm. I can make more friends which have the same hobby with me.

3. How easy or difficult was it for you to make friends when you first arrived? Why?
Very easy, because the campus is small, and people will go around, so its very easy to make friend. (also with imt and clubs)

Yeah, I think I do

Both, if they match my hobbies, and happy and willing to communicate with.

Sometimes, espically in academic life.

A selection, I can go well with some of them

No, I think they are two different circustance
